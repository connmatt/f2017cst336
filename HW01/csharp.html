Video game development is the process of creating video games.


The process of Game Development goes from an idea or concept -> artwork and design ->development -> deployment -> publish.

Game development can go from AAA titles for PC and Consoles to indie games that some people can make on their own.

Its a lot easier today for anyone to develop their own game while all the tools are out there on the internet.

There are free Engines out there that helps with creating your own game.

Unity is one of the free engines that people can downlaod and use.

Unity can do either 2D or 3D game development and can easily implement sprites and objects using C# language.
